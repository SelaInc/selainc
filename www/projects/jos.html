INTERACTIVE DIGITAL MAP

Design

Interactive Digital Map of the University of Jos campus that was part of an induction package available to all new student visiting the school campus. The map is designed to show directions from the main gate to every department and faculty. It was so successful that a request was made to design a version 2.0 in cooperating another page just for induction day, directing all new student through all stages of induction in and around the campus. Built with Sketch up, Flash & Action Script.
